This website is designed to serve as an educational resource for Mrs. Mills, the Middle School and High School Choral Music Director at Fairfield Area School District. Its primary purpose is to support and enhance her students' musical education by offering a centralized platform for supplementary learning materials.

The site will feature a variety of instructional content, including lesson materials, interactive exercises, educational games, and curated external resources. These tools will help students reinforce key musical concepts, practice their skills, and engage more deeply with topics such as music theory, choral techniques, and the instruments Mrs. Mills teaches.

In addition to educational support, the website will also function as an advocacy platform to promote the value of music education within the school community. By combining accessible resources with engaging activities, the site aims to foster a richer, more interactive learning experience for all students.

With a passion for inspiring young musicians, I have built a dynamic career as a dedicated music educator. I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Music Education from York College of Pennsylvania and a Master of Science in Education from Wilkes University. My teaching journey began at Big Spring School District, where I taught elementary music for grades K-5 and directed four elementary choral ensembles, cultivating foundational musical skills and a love for performance in my students.

Currently, I teach at Fairfield Area Middle and High School, where I guide students through a wide variety of musical experiences. My teaching includes general music for grades 5-8, as well as high school electives such as Piano, Ukulele/Guitar, Music Technology, Vocal Music Studio, Music Appreciation, and Cultural Explorations in Music. Beyond the classroom, I direct choral ensembles for grades 5-12 and lead multiple extracurricular groups, including the High School Musical, Show Choir, Chamber Singers, and Select Chorus at the middle school level.

Throughout my career, I have been committed to creating engaging, inclusive, and meaningful musical opportunities that empower students to develop both their artistic talents and their confidence.
